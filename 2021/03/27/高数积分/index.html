不定积分

（一）含有 ax+bax+bax+b 的积分

  1. ∫dxax+b=1a⋅ln∣ax+b∣+C\int \frac{dx}{ax+b} = \frac{1}{a} \cdot \ln{\vert ax+b \vert} +C∫​ax+b​​dx​​=​a​​1​​⋅ln∣ax+b∣+C

∫dxax+b=1a⋅∫d(ax+b)ax+b=1a⋅ln∣ax+b∣+C\begin{aligned} \int \frac{dx}{ax+b} &=\frac{1}{a} \cdot \int \frac{d \left(ax+b \right)}{ax+b}\\ &=\frac{1}{a} \cdot \ln{\vert ax+b \vert} +C \end{aligned} ​∫​ax+b​​dx​​​​​​=​a​​1​​⋅∫​ax+b​​d(ax+b)​​​=​a​​1​​⋅ln∣ax+b∣+C​​

2.\int \left( ax+b \right)^\mu dx = \frac{1}{a(\mu +1)}( ax+b )^{\mu +1} +C (\mu\not= -1)

∫(ax+b)μdx=1a⋅∫(ax+b)μd(ax+b)=1a⋅(ax+b)μ+1μ+1+C\begin{aligned} \int \left( ax+b \right)^{\mu} dx &= \frac{1}{a} \cdot \int \left( ax+b \right)^{\mu}d\left( ax+b \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a} \cdot \frac{\left( ax+b \right)^{\mu +1}}{\mu +1}+C\\ \end{aligned} ​∫(ax+b)​μ​​dx​​​​​=​a​​1​​⋅∫(ax+b)​μ​​d(ax+b)​=​a​​1​​⋅​μ+1​​(ax+b)​μ+1​​​​+C​​

3.∫xax+bdx=1a2(ax+b−bln∣ax+b∣)+C\int \frac{x}{ax+b}dx=\frac{1}{a^2}(ax+b-b\ln\vert ax+b\vert)+C∫​ax+b​​x​​dx=​a​2​​​​1​​(ax+b−bln∣ax+b∣)+C

∫xax+bdx=1a∫ax+b−bax+bdx=1a(∫dx−∫bax+bdx)=1a(∫dx−ba∫d(ax+b)ax+bdx)=1a(x−baln∣ax+b∣)+C=1a2(ax+b−bln∣ax+b∣)+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{x}{ax+b}dx &= \frac{1}{a}\int\frac{ax+b-b}{ax+b}dx\\ &=\frac{1}{a}\left( \int dx -\int \frac{b}{ax+b}dx \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a}\left( \int dx - \frac{b}{a}\int \frac{d(ax+b)}{ax+b}dx \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a}(x-\frac{b}{a}\ln{\vert ax+b\vert}) +C\\ &=\frac{1}{a^2}(ax+b-b\ln\vert ax+b\vert)+C \end{aligned} ​∫​ax+b​​x​​dx​​​​​​​=​a​​1​​∫​ax+b​​ax+b−b​​dx​=​a​​1​​(∫dx−∫​ax+b​​b​​dx)​=​a​​1​​(∫dx−​a​​b​​∫​ax+b​​d(ax+b)​​dx)​=​a​​1​​(x−​a​​b​​ln∣ax+b∣)+C​=​a​2​​​​1​​(ax+b−bln∣ax+b∣)+C​​

4.∫x2ax+bdx=1a3(12(ax+b)2−2b(ax+b)+b2⋅ln∣ax+b∣)+C\int \frac{x^2}{ax+b}dx = \frac{1}{a^3}\left(\frac{1}{2}(ax+b)^2-2b(ax+b)+b^2\cdot\ln\vert ax+b\vert\right)+C∫​ax+b​​x​2​​​​dx=​a​3​​​​1​​(​2​​1​​(ax+b)​2​​−2b(ax+b)+b​2​​⋅ln∣ax+b∣)+C

∫x2ax+bdx=1a3∫(ax+b)2−2b(ax+b)+b2ax+bd(ax+b)=1a3(∫(ax+b)d(ax+b)−∫(2b)d(ax+b)+∫b2ax+bd(ax+b))=1a3(∫(ax+b)d(ax+b)−2b∫d(ax+b)+b2∫d(ax+b)ax+b)=1a3((ax+b)22−2b(ax+b)+b2ln∣ax+b∣)+C\begin{aligned} \int \frac{x^2}{ax+b}dx &= \frac{1}{a^3}\int\frac{(ax+b)^2-2b(ax+b)+b^2}{ax+b}d(ax+b)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( \int(ax+b)d(ax+b)-\int(2b)d(ax+b)+\int\frac{b^2}{ax+b}d(ax+b) \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( \int(ax+b)d(ax+b)-2b\int d(ax+b)+b^2\int\frac{d(ax+b)}{ax+b} \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( \frac{(ax+b)^2}{2}-2b(ax+b)+b^2\ln{\vert ax+b\vert} \right)+C \end{aligned} ​∫​ax+b​​x​2​​​​dx​​​​​​=​a​3​​​​1​​∫​ax+b​​(ax+b)​2​​−2b(ax+b)+b​2​​​​d(ax+b)​=​a​3​​​​1​​(∫(ax+b)d(ax+b)−∫(2b)d(ax+b)+∫​ax+b​​b​2​​​​d(ax+b))​=​a​3​​​​1​​(∫(ax+b)d(ax+b)−2b∫d(ax+b)+b​2​​∫​ax+b​​d(ax+b)​​)​=​a​3​​​​1​​(​2​​(ax+b)​2​​​​−2b(ax+b)+b​2​​ln∣ax+b∣)+C​​

5.∫dxx(ax+b)=−1bln∣ax+bx∣+C\int\frac{dx}{x(ax+b)}=-\frac{1}{b}\ln{\vert\frac{ax+b}{x}\vert}+C∫​x(ax+b)​​dx​​=−​b​​1​​ln∣​x​​ax+b​​∣+C

∫dxx(ax+b)=∫(1bx+−abax+b)dx=1b∫1xdx−ab∫1ax+bdx=1b∫1xdx−1b∫1ax+bd(ax+b)=1b(ln∣x∣−ln∣ax+b∣)+C=1b(ln∣xax+b∣)+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{dx}{x(ax+b)}&=\int\left( \frac{\frac{1}{b}}{x}+\frac{-\frac{a}{b}}{ax+b} \right)dx\\ &=\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{x}dx-\frac{a}{b}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}dx\\ &=\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{x}dx-\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}d(ax+b)\\ &=\frac{1}{b}\left( \ln{\vert x \vert}-\ln{\vert ax+b\vert} \right)+C\\ &=\frac{1}{b}\left( \ln{\vert\frac{x}{ax+b}\vert} \right)+C \end{aligned} ​∫​x(ax+b)​​dx​​​​​​​​​=∫(​x​​​b​​1​​​​+​ax+b​​−​b​​a​​​​)dx​=​b​​1​​∫​x​​1​​dx−​b​​a​​∫​ax+b​​1​​dx​=​b​​1​​∫​x​​1​​dx−​b​​1​​∫​ax+b​​1​​d(ax+b)​=​b​​1​​(ln∣x∣−ln∣ax+b∣)+C​=​b​​1​​(ln∣​ax+b​​x​​∣)+C​​

6.∫dxx2(ax+b)=−1bx+ab2ln∣ax+bx∣+C\int\frac{dx}{x^2(ax+b)}=-\frac{1}{bx}+\frac{a}{b^2}\ln{\vert \frac{ax+b}{x} \vert}+C∫​x​2​​(ax+b)​​dx​​=−​bx​​1​​+​b​2​​​​a​​ln∣​x​​ax+b​​∣+C

∫dxx2(ax+b)=∫(−ab2x+1bx2+a2b2ax+b)dx=−ab2∫1xdx+1b∫1x2dx+a2b2∫1ax+bdx=−ab2∫1xdx+1b∫1x2dx+ab2∫1ax+bd(ax+b)=−ab2ln∣x∣−1b1x+ab2ln∣ax+b∣+C=−1bx+ab2ln∣ax+bx∣+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{dx}{x^2(ax+b)}&=\int\left( \frac{-\frac{a}{b^2}}{x}+\frac{\frac{1}{b}}{x^2}+\frac{\frac{a^2}{b^2}}{ax+b} \right)dx\\ &=-\frac{a}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{x}dx+\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{x^2}dx+\frac{a^2}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}dx\\ &=-\frac{a}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{x}dx+\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{x^2}dx+\frac{a}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}d(ax+b)\\ &=-\frac{a}{b^2}\ln{\vert x\vert}-\frac{1}{b}\frac{1}{x}+\frac{a}{b^2}\ln{\vert ax+b\vert}+C\\ &=-\frac{1}{bx}+\frac{a}{b^2}\ln{\vert \frac{ax+b}{x}\vert}+C \end{aligned} ​∫​x​2​​(ax+b)​​dx​​​​​​​​​=∫(​x​​−​b​2​​​​a​​​​+​x​2​​​​​b​​1​​​​+​ax+b​​​b​2​​​​a​2​​​​​​)dx​=−​b​2​​​​a​​∫​x​​1​​dx+​b​​1​​∫​x​2​​​​1​​dx+​b​2​​​​a​2​​​​∫​ax+b​​1​​dx​=−​b​2​​​​a​​∫​x​​1​​dx+​b​​1​​∫​x​2​​​​1​​dx+​b​2​​​​a​​∫​ax+b​​1​​d(ax+b)​=−​b​2​​​​a​​ln∣x∣−​b​​1​​​x​​1​​+​b​2​​​​a​​ln∣ax+b∣+C​=−​bx​​1​​+​b​2​​​​a​​ln∣​x​​ax+b​​∣+C​​

7.∫x(ax+b)2dx=1a2(ln∣ax+b∣+bax+b)+C\int\frac{x}{(ax+b)^2}dx=\frac{1}{a^2}\left(\ln{\vert ax+b\vert}+\frac{b}{ax+b}\right)+C∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​x​​dx=​a​2​​​​1​​(ln∣ax+b∣+​ax+b​​b​​)+C

∫x(ax+b)2dx=1a∫ax+b−b(ax+b)2dx=1a(∫1ax+bdx−b(ax+b)2dx)=1a(1a∫1ax+bd(ax+b)−ba∫1(ax+b)2d(ax+b))=1a2(ln∣ax+b∣+bax+b)+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{x}{(ax+b)^2}dx&=\frac{1}{a}\int\frac{ax+b-b}{(ax+b)^2}dx\\ &=\frac{1}{a}\left( \int\frac{1}{ax+b}dx-\frac{b}{(ax+b)^2}dx \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a}\left( \frac{1}{a}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}d(ax+b)- \frac{b}{a}\int\frac{1}{(ax+b)^2}d(ax+b)\right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^2}\left( \ln{\vert ax+b\vert}+\frac{b}{ax+b} \right)+C \end{aligned} ​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​x​​dx​​​​​​=​a​​1​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​ax+b−b​​dx​=​a​​1​​(∫​ax+b​​1​​dx−​(ax+b)​2​​​​b​​dx)​=​a​​1​​(​a​​1​​∫​ax+b​​1​​d(ax+b)−​a​​b​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​1​​d(ax+b))​=​a​2​​​​1​​(ln∣ax+b∣+​ax+b​​b​​)+C​​

8.∫x2(ax+b)2dx=1a3(ax+b−2bln∣ax+b∣−b2ax+b)+C\int\frac{x^2}{(ax+b)^2}dx=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( ax+b-2b\ln{\vert ax+b\vert}-\frac{b^2}{ax+b} \right)+C∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​x​2​​​​dx=​a​3​​​​1​​(ax+b−2bln∣ax+b∣−​ax+b​​b​2​​​​)+C

∫x2(ax+b)2dx=1a2∫(ax+b)2−2b(ax+b)+b2(ax+b)2dx=1a3∫(ax+b)2−2b(ax+b)+b2(ax+b)2d(ax+b)=1a3(∫d(ax+b)−∫2bax+bd(ax+b)+∫b2(ax+b)2d(ax+b))=1a3(ax+b−2bln∣ax+b∣−b2ax+b)+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{x^2}{(ax+b)^2}dx&=\frac{1}{a^2}\int\frac{(ax+b)^2-2b(ax+b)+b^2}{(ax+b)^2}dx\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\int\frac{(ax+b)^2-2b(ax+b)+b^2}{(ax+b)^2}d(ax+b)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( \int d(ax+b)-\int\frac{2b}{ax+b}d(ax+b)+\int\frac{b^2}{(ax+b)^2}d(ax+b) \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{a^3}\left( ax+b-2b\ln{\vert ax+b\vert-\frac{b^2}{ax+b}} \right)+C \end{aligned} ​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​x​2​​​​dx​​​​​​=​a​2​​​​1​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​(ax+b)​2​​−2b(ax+b)+b​2​​​​dx​=​a​3​​​​1​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​(ax+b)​2​​−2b(ax+b)+b​2​​​​d(ax+b)​=​a​3​​​​1​​(∫d(ax+b)−∫​ax+b​​2b​​d(ax+b)+∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​b​2​​​​d(ax+b))​=​a​3​​​​1​​(ax+b−2bln∣ax+b∣−​ax+b​​b​2​​​​)+C​​

9.∫dxx(ax+b)2=1b(ax+b)−1b2ln∣ax+bx∣+C\int\frac{dx}{x(ax+b)^2}=\frac{1}{b(ax+b)}-\frac{1}{b^2}\ln{\vert \frac{ax+b}{x}\vert}+C∫​x(ax+b)​2​​​​dx​​=​b(ax+b)​​1​​−​b​2​​​​1​​ln∣​x​​ax+b​​∣+C

∫dxx(ax+b)2=∫(1b2x+−ab2ax+b+−ab(ax+b)2)dx=1b2∫1xdx−ab2∫1ax+bdx−ab∫1(ax+b)2dx=1b2∫1xdx−1b2∫1ax+bd(ax+b)−1b∫1(ax+b)2d(ax+b)=1b2ln∣x∣−1b2ln∣ax+b∣+1b⋅1ax+b+C=1b(ax+b)−1b2ln∣ax+bx∣+C\begin{aligned} \int\frac{dx}{x(ax+b)^2}&=\int\left( \frac{\frac{1}{b^2}}{x} +\frac{-\frac{a}{b^2}}{ax+b}+\frac{-\frac{a}{b}}{(ax+b)^2}\right)dx\\ &=\frac{1}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{x}dx-\frac{a}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}dx-\frac{a}{b}\int\frac{1}{(ax+b)^2}dx\\ &=\frac{1}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{x}dx-\frac{1}{b^2}\int\frac{1}{ax+b}d(ax+b)-\frac{1}{b}\int\frac{1}{(ax+b)^2}d(ax+b)\\ &=\frac{1}{b^2}\ln{\vert x\vert}-\frac{1}{b^2}\ln{\vert ax+b \vert}+\frac{1}{b}\cdot\frac{1}{ax+b}+C\\ &=\frac{1}{b(ax+b)}-\frac{1}{b^2}\ln{\vert \frac{ax+b}{x}\vert}+C \end{aligned} ​∫​x(ax+b)​2​​​​dx​​​​​​​​​=∫(​x​​​b​2​​​​1​​​​+​ax+b​​−​b​2​​​​a​​​​+​(ax+b)​2​​​​−​b​​a​​​​)dx​=​b​2​​​​1​​∫​x​​1​​dx−​b​2​​​​a​​∫​ax+b​​1​​dx−​b​​a​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​1​​dx​=​b​2​​​​1​​∫​x​​1​​dx−​b​2​​​​1​​∫​ax+b​​1​​d(ax+b)−​b​​1​​∫​(ax+b)​2​​​​1​​d(ax+b)​=​b​2​​​​1​​ln∣x∣−​b​2​​​​1​​ln∣ax+b∣+​b​​1​​⋅​ax+b​​1​​+C​=​b(ax+b)​​1​​−​b​2​​​​1​​ln∣​x​​ax+b​​∣+C​​
